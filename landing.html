About Me Get to Know me.
Software Engineer & CTO at Wallbit based in Buenos Aires, Argentina
Hi, I'm Martín Sebastián Tito Gira, an engineer passionate about technology and the development of innovative solutions. I began my career as a developer in 2018 and am currently the CTO at Wallbit, the largest and most challenging project of my current trajectory.
Wallbit is a neobanking platform designed for remote workers, allowing them to have a US account from Latin America, offering them a card and the ability to invest in US stocks. Thanks to this project, I had the opportunity to participate in YCombinator, one of the most prestigious startup accelerators in the world.
